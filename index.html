Privacy Policy for Pathbreakers

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Pathbreakers ("we," "us," or "our") collects and uses information when you play our game.

1. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

For users on iOS 14.5 and later, we respect the Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. Upon launch, we will ask for your permission to track your activity. We use this tracking to provide personalized advertising and measure ad performance. If you choose "Ask App Not to Track," we will respect your decision and will not access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA).

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information (like your name, email, or phone number) directly. However, we use third-party partners who collect data automatically:

  • Identifiers: Our ad partners collect your Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) or other device-specific IDs for advertising and analytics purposes.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you interact with ads (impressions, clicks) and your progress within the game (levels reached).

3. Third-Party Partners

We use the following third-party SDKs to provide advertising and mediation services:

  • TradPlus: Ad mediation platform.
  • AppLovin, UnityAds, Meta, AdMob: Ad networks provided via TradPlus.

These partners may collect, link, and use your data according to their own privacy policies for the purpose of personalized advertising.

4. Data Storage

Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not store your gameplay data on our own servers.
